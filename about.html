Company History & Solar Expertise

SLK Technologies was founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize the renewable energy landscape in South Africa. Starting as a small solar installation company, we have grown into a trusted name in the solar industry. Over nearly a decade, we've installed hundreds of solar panel systems, helped countless businesses achieve energy independence from Eskom, and contributed to reducing carbon emissions across multiple communities throughout Gauteng and beyond. Our journey reflects the rapid growth and innovation in solar technology, from basic grid-tied systems to advanced hybrid solutions with battery storage and smart monitoring.


Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of South Africa's renewable energy transformation, helping customers combat load shedding while saving on electricity costs. Our team of certified solar installers has completed residential, commercial, and industrial solar projects across Johannesburg and surrounding areas, earning a reputation for quality workmanship, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service.
